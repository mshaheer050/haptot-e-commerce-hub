Premium kids brand design system for Haptot ecommerce store.

## Design Tokens
- Primary: rich purple `262 83% 58%` (#7C3AED)
- Secondary: warm orange `24 95% 64%` (#F97316)
- Accent: playful pink `340 75% 65%` (#E8557A)
- Background: pure white `0 0% 100%`
- Foreground: dark `240 20% 16%`
- Rating stars: gold `42 95% 55%`
- Announcement bar: dark purple gradient

## Fonts
- Display: Nunito (800/900 for headings)
- Body: DM Sans (400-700)

## Design Style
- Premium gradients (purple→pink, orange→pink hero)
- Glassmorphism navbar (backdrop-blur)
- Rounded corners (card=20px, btn=14px, radius=0.875rem)
- Soft shadows with purple tint
- Blob animations, float animations
- Pastel surface colors for category cards and trust badges

## Layout
- Announcement bar (marquee) → Glass Navbar → Content → Footer
- Hero: gradient-hero with blob shapes and floating emoji icons
- Category grid: 3-col cards with gradient corners
- Product cards: hover zoom, gradient badges, wishlist heart
- Trust bar with pastel icon backgrounds
- Stats strip with gradient-hero bg
- Newsletter in gradient-hero card
- Footer with gradient top border

## Components
- AnnouncementBar: marquee on gradient-hero
- HeroBanner: gradient bg with blob shapes, dual CTA buttons
- CustomerReviews: gradient rating bar, quote cards
- ProductCard: gradient discount badge, glass wishlist, hover overlay
- WhatsApp: gradient green with pulse-glow animation
